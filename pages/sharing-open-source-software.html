Recently, I've been working with two open-source tools for industrial electricity: CADe SIMU and QElectroTech. CADe SIMU is excellent for creating and simulating electrical control circuits, featuring capabilities for motor control, PLC, GRAFCET, and LADDER diagrams.

QElectroTech has an extensive library covering electrical, hydraulic, and pneumatic components. A standout feature of QElectroTech is the ability to create custom symbols, enhancing the flexibility in design.

Both tools are invaluable for students and professionals alike in our industry.
