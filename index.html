My professional journey is Based in a formal education in industrial electricity at ISTA Beni Mellal, enriched by a self-directed pursuit of computer science knowledge. This unique combination of skills enables me to offer a comprehensive suite of services that bridge the gap between traditional electrical systems and modern digital solutions.

For me, working with electricity and software is more than a profession—it's a calling that inspires my daily life. My approach is fueled by a relentless curiosity about the capabilities of electrical systems and the transformative power of software. In these fields, I see endless opportunities for innovation that can improve our lives and drive progress.

My commitment to this craft extends beyond the technical aspects; it is about understanding and fulfilling the real-world needs of individuals and businesses alike.
