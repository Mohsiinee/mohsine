About me

In an ever-changing, increasingly complex world, I'm driven by the passion for electricity and computer

My name is Mohsine, an industrial electrician and self-taught software developer based in Beni Mellal, Morocco. With formal education in industrial electricity and years of independent exploration of computer science, I bring a unique blend of skills that are as diverse as they are complementary.

It's not merely a job to pay the bills; it's a vocation that fuels my soul. My endless curiosity is drawn to the endless potential of electrical systems and the transformative reach of computer science — two realms where calculated innovation can truly reshape our world.

The core of my professional mission is to leverage the power of electrical energy and computer science to create innovative solutions that make businesses more efficient, homes more comfortable, and technologies more accessible.

From optimizing industrial operations to developing intuitive software, my mission is to elevate standards, exceed expectations, and contribute to a better, more sustainable future for all.
