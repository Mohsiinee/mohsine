About me

In an ever-changing, increasingly complex world, I'm driven by an insatiable passion for electricity and computer science.

My name is Mohsine, an industrial electrician and self-taught computer scientist based in the heart of Morocco, Beni Mellal. With formal education in industrial electricity and years of independent exploration in the realm of computer science, I bring a unique blend of skills that are as diverse as they are complementary.

It's not merely a job to pay the bills; it's a vocation that fuels my soul. My endless curiosity is drawn to the endless potential of electrical systems and the transformative reach of computer science — two realms where calculated innovation can truly reshape our world.

The core of my professional mission is simple yet transformative: to leverage the power of electrical energy and computer science to create innovative solutions that make businesses more efficient, homes more comfortable, and technologies more accessible.


I build partnerships that are founded on trust, fueled by innovation, and aimed at delivering high-impact results.


From optimizing industrial operations to developing intuitive software, my mission is to elevate standards, exceed expectations, and contribute to a better, more sustainable future for all.
